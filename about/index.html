Resume

ChenHaoze, Ph.D
Male 02.03.1995 Hebei
Now studying at the School of Instrument and Electronics, North University of China, Taiyuan, Shanxi
Major: NDT&E
He was awarded the National Scholarship for PhD students in 2021 and selected as the typical representative of 100 National Scholarship winning students in Shanxi Province for the academic year 2020-2021; visited and exchanged with the University of Texas at Arlington in 2019. third place in IEEE ISBI MEMENTO Challenge, Prediction Task 3. During his PhD, he published 5 SCI papers, 1 EI paper, 1 Chinese core paper and 2 conference papers as the first author, attended IEEE International Conference on Instrumentation and Measurement Technology (I2MTC), Far East NDT Forum and made online presentations.
