不锈钢板缺陷深度识别

基于超参数优化机器学习的不锈钢板缺陷深度识别

摘 要：作为无损检测的重要组成部分，红外热成像检测被广泛应用于工业发展的各个领域，用于监测金属零件的质量。为了解决不锈钢工件表面缺陷深度识别精度差，人工检测效率低的问题。提出了基于超参数优化的机器学习分类器的方法实现不锈钢板表面缺陷深度识别。首先从红外热像图中提取鲁棒性高的时序温度特征，随后利用随机搜索和网格搜索方法对最邻近节点算法和支持向量机两种机器学习算法进行超参数搜索。超参数优化方法可以大大缩短缺陷深度识别的时间。实验表明，采用最邻近节点算法作为分类器时，在不锈钢表面缺陷深度的识别中，可以在1.8s内实现80%的缺陷深度识别准确率；采用支持向量机作为分类器时，可以在10s内实现85.7%的缺陷深度识别准确率。极大提高了检测效率。
关键词：无损检测；红外热成像；超参数优化；机器学习；

Depth identification of stainless steel plate defects based on neural network framework search

Abstract: As an important part of non-destructive testing, infrared thermography method is widely used in various fields of industrial development for monitoring the quality of metal parts. In order to solve the problems of poor accuracy of surface defect depth recognition of stainless steel workpiece and low efficiency of manual inspection. We propose the method of machine learning classifier based on hyperparameter optimization to achieve the depth recognition of surface defects of stainless steel plate. Firstly, the robust temporal temperature features are extracted from the infrared thermogram, followed by hyperparametric search using random search and grid search methods for two machine learning algorithms, the nearest neighbor node algorithm and support vector machine. The hyperparametric optimization method can greatly reduce the time of defect depth identification. Experiments show that when the nearest neighbor node algorithm is used as the classifier, 80% defect depth recognition accuracy can be achieved in 1.8s for stainless steel surface defect depth recognition; when the support vector machine is used as the classifier, 85.7% defect depth recognition accuracy can be achieved in 10s. The detection efficiency is greatly improved.
Keywords: non-destructive testing; infrared thermography; hyperparameter optimization; machine learning
